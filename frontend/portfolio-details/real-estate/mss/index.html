MSS - real value properties

Complete website design and social media management strategy to build brand identity and engage a wider audience online.

Project Overview

Client

MSS

Services

Website, SMM

Duration

August 2023 – Present

Challenges

  • Outdated and inconsistent digital presence with no centralized website for business information.
  • Poor social media engagement due to irregular posting and unstructured content themes.
  • No audience targeting or ad strategy in place to boost page visibility and follower base.
  • Lack of brand-aligned visual identity and storytelling across digital platforms.

Solutions

  • Developed a modern, mobile-friendly, and SEO-ready website with clear service listings, brand story, and contact options.
  • Established an organized content calendar for social media with themed posts, client stories, and visual highlights.
  • Created audience-specific content campaigns aimed at growing followers and increasing post engagement.
  • Integrated analytics tools on both website and social pages for performance tracking and strategy refinement.
  • Enhanced visual identity with custom-designed creatives and cohesive brand templates for online use.
  • Delivered monthly social media insights and website analytics reports with content and engagement optimization recommendations.
